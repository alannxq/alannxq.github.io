Burtle guide

This will be a very simple and small guide for using Burtle.


Step 1: Import burtle

Both "BScreen" and "done" aren't necessary, but they will make it easier for us later


Step 2: Create a burtle!

Here we made our own burtle, and we named it "ball."

Your own images go into the brackets after "Burtle", white_circle is a default preset though

Give your burtle a friendly name such as "ball", it will reduce confusion later on


Step 3: Make it move

It's as simple as it seems, just specify the direction and the amount you want to move by. You can animate your burtle by running this command in a loop!

This ".go()" method is one of many which you can find here, along with descriptions of what they do


Step 4: Make the window stay open

That's it, a simple "done()" will prevent your window from closing
